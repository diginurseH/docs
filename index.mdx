# 👩🏽‍⚕️ Welcome to Nursaera AI Documentation

**Nursaera AI** is a nurse-led, open-source health assistant built to support documentation, chronic care, and AI-driven decision support for underserved communities across Africa and beyond.

We believe in empowering frontline health workers with digital tools that are:
- 🌿 Rooted in compassion
- 🧠 Powered by intelligence
- 🪷 Designed for real-life community settings

## 🌍 Why We Exist

In a world where nurses are often overburdened and under-resourced, Nursaera exists to amplify their voice and ease their workflow. This is more than a tool — it’s a movement to democratize healthcare knowledge and put it into the hands of those who serve most intimately.
